by Bishop. Daniel E. Flores

It is with much appreciation and gratitude to the Lord that I remember Shalom Media and its dedicated ministers, supporters, and benefactors. Having the dream of world evangelization and running purely on God’s providence, I am confident that this ministry does wonders around the world. Being its Patron, I am close to the manifold activities they are undertaking globally. Having their international headquarters in McAllen, Texas, is spiritually energizing to the people of the Rio Grande Valley, especially through the much appreciated television network, Shalom World .

Its programs rejuvenate a love for the Church and her people, especially for those who have never experienced or have been away from God and His great love. With secular media constantly suppressing the Church’s voice and manipulating her truths, Shalom World has emerged as a true voice of the Church.

The strength and source of the growth of Shalom Media is that this is a ministry that stands with the Church. The ministry-run conferences and publications and other media initiatives are real opportunities of bringing forth spiritual energy rooted in prayer.

The backbone of the ministry is the tens of thousands of individuals around the world who constitute the Shalom Peace Fellowship. These Fellowship members support the ministry financially, but most especially, through prayer. With God’s providence—and no commercial backing—Shalom Media continues to be fruitful. May God reward and abundantly bless all those who support this ministry, in order that it fulfills its mission in God’s plan.

I hope and pray that Shalom Media serves as a source of inspiration for many to spread the Good News of Jesus Christ throughout the world.

Patron, Shalom Media USA
